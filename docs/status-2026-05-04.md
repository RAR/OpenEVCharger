## C. TLV protocol surface

Spec called for 12 commands + 6 events. Shipped: **20 commands + 19 events** (RFID +
lifetime/build/device-id/auth-config extensions). STATE_REPORT is at 56 B =
TLV_PAYLOAD_MAX cap; future fields need a payload-cap bump or a side-channel event.

## D. Open carve-outs

- `FAULT_DIODE_CHECK` ‚Äî enum-only, blocked on the bench 5-point negative-half cal.
- `FAULT_CC_OUT_OF_RANGE` ‚Äî decoder live; detector raise gated on
  `OPENBHZD_CC_DETECTOR=1` until bench cal of the OEM CC divider
  (bench unit reads PA7 raw‚âà12 with no plug, M2 sanity recorded raw=4095,
  topology inconsistent until characterised).
- `FAULT_HARD/SOFT_OC`, `FAULT_RELAY_WELD/STUCK_OPEN` ‚Äî cal-gated on BL0939 IA scale.
- `FAULT_GFCI_SELF_TEST` ‚Äî wired (`gfci_self_test()` polarity-agnostic CAL pulse, `self_test_gfci_cal()` boot wrapper); raise gated behind `OPENBHZD_GFCI_CAL_SELF_TEST=1` until bench validates the PE3-CAL‚ÜíPE2-sense path on this PCB.
- Relay actuate-readback gated by `OPENBHZD_RELAY_ACTUATE_SELF_TEST = 0`.
- ~~cp_low ETSIC trigger swap doesn't fire on this GD32F205 ‚Äî try ADC1-dedicated next.~~ **Fixed:** ADC1-dedicated path with TIMER0 CH3 trigger and CCxE=ENABLE captures cp_low cleanly (12000 mV in idle_high as expected).
